Because building confidence is core to Microsoft business leadership, culture, and values, developing authentic confidence is critical for employees.

Authentic confidence is confidence that provides balance between self-doubt and arrogance in the face of challenging situations. To build authentic confidence, employees learn to act courageously in the face of challenges. This results in the employees becoming more confident in their own abilities and also in their team or group, and in their ability to positively impact the business and the company.
